# Land of Chaos

## Difficulty & Rewards

| Difficulty                    | Grade  | # of Levels | Reward                                                                                                       |
| ----------------------------- | ------ | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 3-4                           | Yellow | 16          | - Silver totem fragments<br>- Totem essence<br>- Totem fragments<br>- Coins                                  |
| 5-8                           | Yellow | 16          | - Gold totem fragments<br>- Totem essence<br>- Totem fragments<br>- Coins                                    |
| 91-181                        | Red    | 4           | - Gold totem fragments<br>- Totem essence<br>- Totem fragments<br>- Coins                                    |
| Every even level from 182-999 | Red    | 4           | - Gold totem fragments<br>- Totem essence<br>- Totem fragments<br>- Coins<br>- Red diamonds<br>- Totem stone |
| Every odd level from 182-999  | Red    | 4           | - Gold totem fragments<br>- Totem essence<br>- Totem fragments<br>- Coins                                    |

The following totem rewards can be obtained in Land of Chaos:
- Orbis totem fragment
- Sword totem fragment
- Mushroom totem fragment
- Queen totem fragment
Totem rewards repeat for increasing difficulty.

Starting at level 182, players receive 1 totem stone as a reward for defeating the final boss on every even-numbered level.
